About Me

Hi, I'm Kyle.

I live in St. Louis, Missouri.

My focus at university included software engineering and UI/UX design. I started coding and design as hobbies in middle school, and always loved web dev and Adobe's tools. Figma is a go-to of mine.

In my spare time, I work with embedded C and single-board computers. I'm also making gourmet desserts and listening to new music.

I want to find work that offers strong mentorship and a blend of technical and creative challenges. I can see myself one day taking on a more senior engineering role, or doing independent design consulting. I'm always open to new opportunities!
